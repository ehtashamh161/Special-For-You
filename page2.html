MY Dear Friend 💖

Tum sirf friend nahi ho… tum special ho 🤗🔥

Meri pyari dost, Tum sirf ek friend nahi balkay meri behn jesi ho. Allah tumhein hamesha khush rakhe, tumhara naseeb itna acha kare ke zindagi tum par fakhar kare. Har khushi tumhare qadam choome aur har gham tum se door bhaage.

Dua hai ke tumhari zindagi roshniyon se bhari ho, har din tumhare liye nayi umeed aur khubsurat lamhe laaye. Allah tumhein woh sab kuch ata kare jo tumhare dil mein hai, aur tumhein hamesha kamyabi aur sukoon naseeb ho.

Tumhari dosti mere liye ek gift hai, aur main hamesha Allah ka shukar ada karta hoon ke mujhe tum jesi pyari aur sachchi friend mili.

Hamesha aisay hi muskurati raho. Khush raho, abaad raho 💖

Mujhe tumhari friendship bohat pasand hai 💕
